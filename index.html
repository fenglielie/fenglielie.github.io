交错网格上的DG格式2

Posted on 2022-07-25 In learn Symbols count in article: 6.9k Reading time ≈ 7 mins.

交错网格的DG格式求解不可压N-S方程组。

参考:

Tavelli M, Dumbser M. A staggered semi-implicit discontinuous Galerkin method for the two dimensional incompressible Navier–Stokes equations[J]. Applied Mathematics and Computation, 2014, 248: 70-92.

Tavelli M, Dumbser M. A staggered space–time discontinuous Galerkin method for the incompressible Navier–Stokes equations on two-dimensional triangular meshes[J]. Computers & Fluids, 2015, 119: 235-249.

1.控制方程

考虑二维不可压N-S方程组如下：

\[ \begin{aligned} \frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t} + (\mathbf{u}\cdot \nabla) \mathbf{u} &= -\nabla p + \nu \nabla^2 \mathbf{u}\\ \nabla \cdot \mathbf{u} &= 0\\ \end{aligned} \]

记\(\mathbf{F}_c\)和\(\mathbf{F}\)如下

\[ \begin{aligned} \mathbf{F}_c &= \mathbf{F}_c(\mathbf{u}) = \mathbf{u} \otimes \mathbf{u} = \begin{pmatrix} u^2 & uv\\ uv & v^2 \end{pmatrix}\\ \mathbf{F} &= \mathbf{F}(\mathbf{u},\nabla \mathbf{u}) = \mathbf{F}_c(\mathbf{u})- \nu \nabla \mathbf{u}= \begin{pmatrix} u^2 - \nu u_x & uv - \nu u_y \\ u v - \nu v_x & v^2 - \nu v_y \end{pmatrix} \end{aligned} \]

从而把动量方程整理为

\[ \frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t} + \nabla \cdot \mathbf{F}+\nabla p = 0 \]

记\(L(\mathbf{u}) = \frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t} + \nabla \cdot \mathbf{F}\)。

2.空间离散——交错网格

主要想法为：从二维区域\(\Omega\)的三角网格剖分出发，构造一个对偶的四边形网格(edge-based)，在原始三角形上定义压强，在四边形上定义速度。

记区域\(\Omega\)被剖分为三角形\(\{T_i,i=1,\cdots,N_i\}\)，共有\(N_i\)个；记边为\(\{\Gamma_j,j=1,\cdots,N_j\}\)，共有\(N_j\)个。 记\(B(\Omega)\)为属于\(\partial\Omega\)的边的指标集，记\(S_i\)为三角形\(T_i\)的三条边的指标集。 对于\(j \notin B(\Omega)\)，边\(\Gamma_j\)被两个三角形共享，对应的指标记作\(l(j),r(j)\)。

对于三角形\(T_i\)，以及它的一条边\(\Gamma_j\)(\(j \in S_i\))，记与\(T_i\)共享\(\Gamma_j\)的三角形指标为\(\rho(i,j)\)。

对于内部边\(\Gamma_j\)，对应有一个四边形单元\(R_j\)，由两侧的小三角形拼成。

如下图，对于三角形\(T_i\)，有三条边\(\{\Gamma_{j_1},\Gamma_{j_2},\Gamma_{j_3}\}\)，取内部一点(重心?)将\(T_i\)分成三个小三角形，记作\(\{T_{i,j_1},T_{i,j_2},T_{i,j_3}\}\)。在\(\Gamma_{j_1}\)侧的相邻三角形为\(T_{i_1}\)，四边形\(R_{j_1}\)由两个小三角形\(T_{i,j_1},T_{i_1,j_1}\)组成。

下文将三角形网格称为原始网格或主网格，将四边形网格称作对偶网格。

在对偶网格上，也有类似的记号： 记四边形的总数为\(N_l\)，记\(\Gamma_l\)为边的编号(不是原三角形的边)，\(S_j\)为\(R_j\)对应的边的指标集，对于\(\Gamma_l\)被两个四边形共享，对应的指标记作\(l_{jl}(l),r_{jl}(l)\)。

基函数

三角形的压强元总是可以变换到标准的直角三角形单元\(T_{std}\)，四边形的速度元总是由两个三角形拼成，可以把它们分别变换到两个直角三角形单元，拼成一个正方形单元\(R_{std}\)。

\[ \begin{aligned} T_{std}&=\{(\xi,\eta)\in R^2 :0\le \xi \le 1,0,\le \eta \le 1 - \xi\}\\ R_{std}&=\{(\xi,\eta)\in R^2:0\le \xi \le 1,0,\le \eta \le 1\}\\ \end{aligned} \]

从参考单元到一般单元的映射记作 \[ \begin{aligned} T_i \overset{T^i}{\to} T_{std}\\ R_j \overset{R^j}{\to} R_{std}\\ \end{aligned} \]

The maps from physical coordinates to reference coordinates can be constructed following a classical sub-parametric or a complete iso-parametric approach.

在直角三角形\(T_{std}\)使用\(P^k\)多项式作为基函数，共\(\frac{(k+1)(k+2)}2\)个； 在正方形\(R_{std}\)使用\(Q^k\)多项式作为基函数，共\((k+1)^2\)个。

取主网格(main grid)的分片多项式空间\(V_h^m\)为

\[ \begin{aligned} V_h^m = \{\phi: \phi|_{T_i} \in P^k(T_i),\forall i\} \end{aligned} \]

取对偶网格(dual grid)的分片多项式空间\(V_h^d\)为

\[ \begin{aligned} V_h^d = \{\psi: \psi|_{R_j} \in Q^k(R_j),\forall j \notin B(\Omega)\} \end{aligned} \]

定义离散的压强\(p_h \in V_h^m\)，离散的速度\(\mathbf{u}_h \in [V_h^d]^2\)，允许它们在每个单元的边界产生间断。

分别用基函数展开，得到系数向量\(P_i(t),U_i(t)\)：

\[ \begin{aligned} p_h(x,y,t)|_{T_i} = \sum_l \phi_l^{(i)}(x,y)\hat{p}_{l,i}(t)= \Phi^{(i)}(x,y) P_i(t) \\ \mathbf{u}_h(x,y,t)|_{R_j} = \sum_l \psi_l^{(j)}(x,y)\hat{\mathbf{u}}_{l,j}(t) =\Psi^{(j)}(x,y) U_j(t) \end{aligned} \]

3.DG格式

对动量方程乘以速度测试函数\(\psi \in [V_h^d]^2\)，在四边形\(R_j\)积分；对连续性方程乘以压强测试函数\(\phi \in V_h^m\)，在三角形\(T_i\)积分，得到下式：

\[ \begin{aligned} \int_{R_j} \mathbf{\psi}^{(j)} \left(\frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t} +\nabla \cdot \mathbf{F} + \nabla p \right) &= 0\\ \int_{T_i} \phi^{(i)} (\nabla \cdot \mathbf{u}) &= 0 \end{aligned} \]

其中第二个式子分部积分即

\[ \begin{aligned} \int_{T_i} \nabla \phi^{(i)} \cdot \mathbf{u} - \int_{\partial T_i} \phi^{(i)} \,\mathbf{u}\cdot \mathbf{n} = 0 \end{aligned} \]

按照小三角形把积分区域拆分(离散的速度会在小三角形之间间断)

\[ \begin{aligned} \sum_{j \in S_i}\left(\int_{T_{ij}} \nabla \phi^{(i)} \cdot \mathbf{u} - \int_{\Gamma_j} \phi^{(i)} \,\mathbf{u}\cdot \mathbf{n}\right) = 0 \end{aligned} \]

对于动量方程，\(R_j\)由两个小三角形\(T_{l(j),j},T_{r(j),j}\)组成，对应主网格的边\(\Gamma_j\)，将压强部分拆开(离散的压强会在\(\Gamma_j\)间断)

\[ \begin{aligned} \int_{R_j} \mathbf{\psi}^{(j)} \left(\frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t} + \nabla \cdot \mathbf{F}\right)+ \int_{T_{l(j),j}}\mathbf{\psi}^{(j)} \nabla p + \int_{T_{r(j),j}}\mathbf{\psi}^{(j)} \nabla p + \int_{\Gamma_j} \mathbf{n}\cdot \mathbf{\psi}^{(j)}(p_{r(j)}-p_{l(j)}) &= 0\\ \end{aligned} \]

对于\(\mathbf{u}_h,p_h\)，代入上式，用基函数展开，得到系数组\(\hat{\mathbf{u}},\hat{p}\)需要满足的方程组：

\[ \begin{aligned} \sum_l \sum_{j \in S_i}\left(\int_{T_{ij}} \nabla \phi^{(i)} \cdot \mathbf{\psi}^{(j)}_l - \int_{\Gamma_j} \phi^{(i)} \,\mathbf{\psi}^{(j)}_l\cdot \mathbf{n} \right)\hat{\mathbf{u}}_{l,j} = 0 \end{aligned} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} \int_{R_j} \mathbf{\psi}^{(j)} \psi_l^{(j)} L_h(\hat{\mathbf{u}}_{l,j}) +\int_{T_{l(j),j}}\mathbf{\psi}^{(j)} \nabla \phi_l^{(l(j))} \hat{p}_{l,l(j)} + \int_{T_{r(j),j}}\mathbf{\psi}^{(j)} \nabla \phi_l^{(r(j))} \hat{p}_{l,r(j)} + \int_{\Gamma_j} \mathbf{n}\cdot \mathbf{\psi}^{(j)} (\phi_l^{(r(j))} \hat{p}_{l,r(j)} - \phi_l^{l(j)} \hat{p}_{l,l(j)}) &= 0\\ \end{aligned} \]

其中\(L(\mathbf{u}) = \frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t} + \nabla \cdot \mathbf{F}\)，\(L_h\)是\(L\)算子的数值近似，见下文。

最终可以整理为如下形式

\[ \begin{aligned} \sum_{j \in S_i} D_{ij}\hat{\mathbf{u}}_j &= 0\\ M_j L_h(\hat{\mathbf{u}}_j) + R_j \hat{p}_{r(j)} - L_j \hat{p}_{l(j)} &=0 \end{aligned} \]

其中\(D_{ij},M_j,R_j,L_j\)都是由网格和基函数所确定的值，并且\(R_j,L_j\)可以被统一为\(Q_{ij}\)，使得

\[ \begin{aligned} L_i = - Q_{l(j),j}, R_i = Q_{r(j),j}\\ \end{aligned} \]

从而得到

\[ \begin{aligned} \sum_{j \in S_i} D_{ij}\hat{\mathbf{u}}_j &= 0\\ M_j L_h(\hat{\mathbf{u}}_j) + Q_{r(j),j} \hat{p}_{r(j)} +Q_{l(j),j} \hat{p}_{l(j)} &=0 \end{aligned} \]

时间离散

对于压强，取常数\(\theta \in [\frac12,1]\)，使得\(p^{n+\theta}=\theta p^{n+1} + (1-\theta)p^n\)，此时

\[ Q_{r(j),j} \hat{p}_{r(j)} +Q_{l(j),j} \hat{p}_{l(j)} \to \theta\left[Q_{r(j),j} \hat{p}^{n+1}_{r(j)} +Q_{l(j),j} \hat{p}^{n+1}_{l(j)}\right]+ (1-\theta)\left[Q_{r(j),j} \hat{p}^n_{r(j)} +Q_{l(j),j} \hat{p}^n_{l(j)}\right] \]

连续性方程的速度取\(n+1\)时刻，即 \[ \begin{aligned} \sum_{j \in S_i} D_{ij}\hat{\mathbf{u}}_j^{n+1} &= 0\\ \end{aligned} \]

对于\(L_h(\hat{\mathbf{u}}_j)\)部分，包含了时间项，对流项和扩散项，如果对流项和扩散项全部采用显式，则可以写成

\[ L_h(\hat{\mathbf{u}}_j) \to \frac{\hat{\mathbf{u}}_j^{n+1}- \widehat{F\mathbf{u}}_j^n}{\Delta t} \]

最终得到

\[ \begin{aligned} \sum_{j \in S_i} D_{ij}\hat{\mathbf{u}}_j^{n+1} &= 0\\ M_j \frac{\hat{\mathbf{u}}_j^{n+1}- \widehat{F\mathbf{u}}_j^n}{\Delta t} + \theta\left[Q_{r(j),j} \hat{p}^{n+1}_{r(j)} +Q_{l(j),j} \hat{p}^{n+1}_{l(j)}\right]+ (1-\theta)\left[Q_{r(j),j} \hat{p}^n_{r(j)} +Q_{l(j),j} \hat{p}^n_{l(j)}\right] &=0 \end{aligned} \]

有

\[ \hat{\mathbf{u}}_j^{n+1}=\widehat{F\mathbf{u}}_j^n -\Delta t M_j^{-1}\left\{ \theta\left[Q_{r(j),j} \hat{p}^{n+1}_{r(j)} +Q_{l(j),j} \hat{p}^{n+1}_{l(j)}\right]+ (1-\theta)\left[Q_{r(j),j} \hat{p}^n_{r(j)} +Q_{l(j),j} \hat{p}^n_{l(j)}\right] \right\} \]

代入连续性方程可得关于\(\hat{p}^{n+1}\)的全局的线性方程组，右端项\(b_i^n\)已知，

\[ \begin{aligned} \sum_{j \in S_i} -\Delta t D_{ij} M_j^{-1} \theta\left[Q_{r(j),j} \hat{p}^{n+1}_{r(j)} +Q_{l(j),j} \hat{p}^{n+1}_{l(j)}\right]= b_i^n\\ b_i^n = \sum_{j\in S_i} -D_{ij}\widehat{F\mathbf{u}}_j^n + (1-\theta)M_j^{-1} \left[Q_{r(j),j} \hat{p}^n_{r(j)} +Q_{l(j),j} \hat{p}^n_{l(j)}\right] \end{aligned} \]

解得压强\(\hat{p}^{n+1}\)后，可代回计算\(\hat{\mathbf{u}}^{n+1}\)
