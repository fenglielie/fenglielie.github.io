LDG——一维对流扩散方程，一维N-S方程组

Posted on 2022-06-27 Edited on 2022-07-07 In code Symbols count in article: 11k Reading time ≈ 11 mins.

LDG练习，一维对流扩散方程和一维可压缩N-S方程组。

一维对流扩散方程

考虑一维的对流扩散方程如下

\[ \begin{aligned} u_t + f(u)_x &= (b(u) u_x)_x\\ u(x,0)&=u_0(x)\\ u(0,t)&=u(2\pi,t)\\ \end{aligned} \]

其中初值\(u_0(x)\)也是\(2\pi\)周期的，\(b(u)\ge 0\)。

LDG方法使用中间变量把对流扩散方程中的高阶导转化为方程组：

\[ \begin{aligned} u_t + f(u,x)_x &= (a(u) q)_x\\ q - g(u)_x &= 0 \end{aligned} \]

其中\(a(u)=\sqrt{b(u)}\)，\(g(u)\)是\(a(u)\)的原函数。

首先给出文章中算例：

  • 算例一(热方程)：方程为\(u_t=u_{xx}\)，精确解为\(u(x,t)=e^{-t}\sin(x)\)；
  • 算例二：方程为\(u_t+u_x=u_{xx}\)，精确解为\(u(x,t)=e^{-t}\sin(x-t)\)。

为了简化模型，我们使用下面形式的方程，其中常数\(\lambda,\mu^2\)待定。\((\mu \ge 0)\)

\[ u_t + \lambda u_x = \mu^2 u_{xx} \]

加入中间变量后变成方程组

\[ \begin{aligned} u_t + f(u)_x &= h(q)_x \\ q - g(u)_x &= 0 \end{aligned} \]

其中\(f(u)=\lambda u\)，\(g(u)=\mu u\)，\(h(q)=\mu q\)。对应两个算例，分别取不同的常数\(\lambda,\mu\)即可。

在一维均匀网格下，DG半离散格式变为：find \(u_h,q_h \in V_h^k\), s.t. \(\forall v_h, w_h \in V_h^k\)

\[ \begin{aligned} \int_{I_j} (u_h)_t v_h - \int_{I_j} f(u_h) (v_h)_x + \left[ \widehat{f}(u_h(x_{j+1/2})) v_h(x_{j+1/2}^-) - \widehat{f}(u_h(x_{j-1/2})) v_h(x_{j-1/2}^+) \right]\\ = - \int_{I_j} h(q_h) (v_h)_x + \left[ \widehat{h}(q_h(x_{j+1/2}))v_h(x_{j+1/2}^-) - \widehat{h}(q_h(x_{j-1/2}))v_h(x_{j-1/2}^+) \right]\\ \int_{I_j} q_h w_h =- \int_{I_j} g(u_h) (w_h)_x + \left[ \widehat{g}(u_h(x_{j+1/2}))w_h(x_{j+1/2}^-)-\widehat{g}(u_h(x_{j-1/2}))w_h(x_{j-1/2}^+) \right] \end{aligned} \]

为了兼容其他格式，我们也把第二个公式再次分部积分进行转化，在使用\(q_h \in V_h^k\)近似\(g(u_h)_x \in V_h^{k-1}\)时，记

\[ q_h = g(u_h)_x + R^w_h + R^e_h \]

其中基于右边界的修正量\(R^e_h\)和基于左边界的修正量\(R^w_h\)分别满足：\(\forall w_h \in V_h^k\)

\[ \begin{aligned} \int_{I_j} R^e_h w_h &= \left[\widehat{g}(u_h(x_{j+1/2})) - g(u_h(x_{j+1/2}^-))\right] w_h(x_{j+1/2}^-)\\ \int_{I_j} R^w_h w_h &= \left[\widehat{g}(u_h(x_{j-1/2})) - g(u_h(x_{j-1/2}^+))\right] w_h(x_{j-1/2}^+)\\ \end{aligned} \]

对时间直接使用向前欧拉离散(实践中使用Runge-Kutta格式替代)，计算流程为：

  1. 计算初值\(u_h^0\)，n=0
  2. 基于\(u_h^n\)，计算修正量\(R_h^{e,n}\)和\(R_h^{w,n}\)，计算\(q_h^n = \mu \left[(u_h^n)_x + R^{w,n}_h + R^{e,n}_h \right]\)
  3. 计算下一步的\(u_h^{n+1}\)，\(n:=n+1\)，回到第二步或者结束计算。

剩下三个数值流通量的选取：

  • \(\widehat{f}(u_h(x^-,x^+))\)：对流项的数值流通量，例如LF流通量；
  • \(\widehat{g}(u_h(x^-,x^+))\)：取作\(\mu u_h(x^-)\)；
  • \(\widehat{h}(q_h(x^-,x^+))\)：取作\(\mu q_h(x^+)\)。(后两个也可以左右互换)

时间步长的选取：如果使用\(\Delta t=\text{CFL} \Delta x\) 可能不行，使用足够小的\(\Delta t=\text{CFL} \Delta x^2\)可以保证稳定性。

参考文献： Y. Cheng and C.-W. Shu, Superconvergence of local discontinuous Galerkin methods for one-dimensional convection-diffusion equations , Computers and Structures, v87 (2009), pp.630-641.

计算结果

对于k=0,1,2，都达到了预期的精度，注意对于k=0,1，使用了\(\Delta t = \frac{0.2}{2k+3} \Delta x^2\)，对于\(k=2\)，因为不稳定，使用了更小的\(\Delta t = \frac{0.1}{2k+3} \Delta x^2\)，终止时间都是\(t=1\)。如下图：

一维N-S方程组

考虑无量纲化的一维的N-S方程组如下，

\[ U_t + F(U)_x = H(U,U_x)_x + S(U) \]

其中\(F(U)_x\)为对流项，\(H(U,U_x)_x\)为黏性项，\(S(U)\)为源项，默认取\(S(U)=0\)。

守恒型变量\(U=[\rho,\rho u,E]^T\)，其中能量

\[ E = \frac{p}{\gamma-1} + \frac12 \rho u^2 \]

对流项

\[ \begin{aligned} F(U)&=[\rho u,\rho u^2 + p,u(E+p)]^T\\ &=[\rho u,\rho u^2 + p, u\left(\frac{\gamma}{\gamma-1}p + \frac12 \rho u^2 \right)]^T \end{aligned} \]

从原始变量\(\{\rho,u,p\}\)转化到守恒型变量\(U=[\xi_1,\xi_2,\xi_3]^T\)：

\[ \begin{aligned} \xi_1 &= \rho \\ \xi_2 &= \rho u\\ \xi_3 &= \frac{p}{\gamma-1}+\frac12 \rho u^2 \end{aligned} \]

从守恒型变量\(U=[\xi_1,\xi_2,\xi_3]^T\)转化到原始变量\(\{\rho,u,p\}\)：

\[ \begin{aligned} \rho &= \xi_1\\ u &= \frac{\xi_2}{\xi_1}\\ p &= (\gamma -1)\left(\xi_3 - \frac12 \frac{\xi_2^2}{\xi_1}\right) \end{aligned} \]

以上都和一维可压缩理想气体相同，不同之处在于黏性项，黏性项具体为：

\[ \begin{aligned} H(U,U_x) &= [0, \tau_{xx},u\tau_{xx} + \frac{1}{Re\,Pr(\gamma-1)}(a^2)_x]^T\\ &= [0, \frac{4}{3Re} u_x, \frac{4}{3Re} u u_x + \frac{\gamma}{Re\,Pr(\gamma-1)}\left(\frac{p_x \rho - p \rho_x}{\rho^2}\right)]^T \end{aligned} \]

我们首先引入中间变量\(V=U_x\)，得到方程组

\[ \begin{aligned} U_t + F(U)_x &= H(U,V)_x + S(U)\\ V &= G(U)_x \end{aligned} \]

其中\(G(U)=U\)，记\(V=[\eta_1,\eta_2,\eta_3]^T\)，从原始变量\(\{\rho,u,p\}\)和\(\{\rho_x,u_x,p_x\}\)转化到\(V=[\eta_1,\eta_2,\eta_3]^T\)：

\[ \begin{aligned} \eta_1 &= \rho_x \\ \eta_2 &= (\rho u)_x = \rho_x u + \rho u_x \\ \eta_3 &= E_x = \frac{p_x}{\gamma-1} + \frac12 \rho_x u^2 + \rho u u_x \end{aligned} \]

从\(U=[\xi_1,\xi_2,\xi_3]^T\)和\(V=[\eta_1,\eta_2,\eta_3]^T\)转化到原始变量\(\{\rho_x,u_x,p_x\}\)：

\[ \begin{aligned} \rho_x &= \eta_1\\ u_x &= \frac{1}{\rho}(\eta_2 - \rho_x u) = \frac{1}{\xi_1}\left(\eta_2 - \eta_1 \frac{\xi_2}{\xi_1} \right)\\ p_x &= (\gamma-1)\left(\eta_3 - \frac12 \rho_x u^2 - \rho u u_x\right)\\ &= (\gamma-1)\left[\eta_3 - \frac12 \eta_1 \left(\frac{\xi_2}{\xi_1}\right)^2 - \xi_2 \frac{1}{\xi_1}\left(\eta_2 - \eta_1 \frac{\xi_2}{\xi_1} \right) \right] \end{aligned} \]

在一维均匀网格下，DG半离散格式变为：find \(U_h,V_h \in V_h^k\), s.t. \(\forall W_h, Y_h \in V_h^k\)

\[ \begin{aligned} \int_{I_j} (U_h)_t W_h - \int_{I_j} F(U_h)(W_h)_x +\widehat{F}(U_h(x_{i+1/2}))W_h(x_{i+1/2}^-) -\widehat{F}(U_h(x_{i-1/2}))W_h(x_{i-1/2}^+)\\ = -\int_{I_j}H(U_h,V_h)(W_h)_x + \widehat{H}(U_h,V_h)(x_{i+1/2})W_h(x_{i+1/2}^-) - \widehat{H}(U_h,V_h)(x_{i-1/2})W_h(x_{i-1/2}^+) + \int_{I_j}S(U_h) W_h \\ \int_{I_j} V_h Y_h = -\int_{I_j}G(U_h)(Y_h)_x + \widehat{G}(U_h)(x_{i+1/2})Y_h(x_{i+1/2}^-) - \widehat{G}(U_h)(x_{i-1/2})Y_h(x_{i-1/2}^+) \end{aligned} \]

一维的线性算例

我们需要一个简单的算例进行精度测试，直接改造原本的Euler方程组的算例，计算区域\([0,2]\)，周期边界。

\[ \begin{aligned} \rho(x,0)&= 1+ 0.2 \sin(\pi x)\\ u(x,0)&= u_0=0.7\\ p(x,0)&= p_0=1 \end{aligned} \]

取常数\(\gamma = 1.4\)，此时对于Euler方程组，恰好时间项和对流项抵消，退化为线性方程，精确解为

\[ \begin{aligned} \rho(x,0)&= 1+ 0.2 \sin(\pi (x- u_0 t))\\ u(x,0)&= u_0 =0.7\\ p(x,0)&= p_0 =1 \end{aligned} \]

因此，我们加上源项，直接和黏性项抵消。对于黏性项，取\(Re=1000\)，\(Pr=0.72\)，则黏性项和源项为

\[ \begin{aligned} H(U,U_x) &= \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 0\\ \kappa \Phi(x,t) \end{pmatrix}\\ S(x,t) &= \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 0\\ -\kappa \Phi(x,t)_x \end{pmatrix} \end{aligned} \]

其中常系数\(\kappa = \frac{\gamma}{Re\,Pr(\gamma-1)}\)，\(\Phi(x,t)_x\)具体为

\[ \begin{aligned} \Phi(x,t)_x &= p_0 \left(\frac{1}{\rho}\right)_{xx} = p_0 \left( \frac{2\rho_x^2}{\rho^3} - \frac{\rho_{xx}}{\rho^2} \right) \end{aligned} \]

计算结果

对于k=0,1,2，都达到了预期的精度，注意对于k=0,1,2，使用了\(\Delta t = \frac{0.5}{2k+3} \left(\frac{\Delta x}{\alpha}\right)^2\)，终止时间\(t=0.5\)。如下图：

二维N-S方程组

二维和一维没有本质区别，只是计算更繁琐了。

考虑无量纲化的二维(可压缩)的N-S方程组如下，

\[ U_t + F_1(U)_x + F_2(U)_y = H_1(U,U_x,U_y)_x + H_2(U,U_x,U_y)_y + S(U) \]

其中\(F_1(U)_x,F_2(U)_y\)为对流项，\(H_1(U,U_x,U_y)_x,H_2(U,U_x,U_y)_y\)为黏性项，\(S(U)\)为源项，默认取\(S(U)=0\)。

守恒型变量\(U=[\rho,\rho u ,\rho v ,E]^T\)，其中能量

\[ E = \frac{p}{\gamma-1} + \frac12 \rho(u^2+v^2) \]

对流项

\[ \begin{aligned} F_1(U) &= [\rho u ,\rho u^2 + p,\rho u v, u(E+p)]^T\\ &= [\rho u,\rho u^2 + p ,\rho u v, u(\frac{\gamma}{\gamma-1} p + \frac12 \rho(u^2+v^2))]^T\\ F_2(U) &= [\rho v ,\rho u v,\rho v^2+ p, v(E+p)]^T\\ &= [\rho v,\rho u v ,\rho v^2 + p, v(\frac{\gamma}{\gamma-1} p + \frac12 \rho(u^2+v^2))]^T \end{aligned} \]

从原始变量\(\{\rho,u,v,p\}\)转化到守恒型变量\(U=[\xi_1,\xi_2,\xi_3,\xi_4]^T\)：

\[ \begin{aligned} \xi_1 &= \rho \\ \xi_2 &= \rho u\\ \xi_3 &= \rho v\\ \xi_4 &= \frac{p}{\gamma-1} + \frac12 \rho(u^2+v^2) \end{aligned} \]

从守恒型变量\(U=[\xi_1,\xi_2,\xi_3,\xi_4]^T\)转化到原始变量\(\{\rho,u,v,p\}\)：

\[ \begin{aligned} \rho &= \xi_1\\ u &= \frac{\xi_2}{\xi_1}\\ v &= \frac{\xi_3}{\xi_1}\\ p &= (\gamma-1)\left(\xi_4 - \frac12 \frac{\xi_2^2 + \xi_3^2}{\xi_1} \right) \end{aligned} \]

以上都和二维可压缩理想气体相同，不同之处在于黏性项，黏性项具体为：

\[ \begin{aligned} H_1(U,U_x,U_y) &= [0,\frac{1}{Re}\tau_{xx},\frac{1}{Re}\tau_{xy},\frac{1}{Re}\epsilon_1 + \frac{1}{Re\,Pr(\gamma-1)} \left(\frac{\gamma p}{\rho}\right)_x ]^T\\ H_2(U,U_x,U_y) &= [0,\frac{1}{Re}\tau_{xy},\frac{1}{Re}\tau_{yy},\frac{1}{Re}\epsilon_2 + \frac{1}{Re\,Pr(\gamma-1)} \left(\frac{\gamma p}{\rho}\right)_y ]^T \end{aligned} \]

其中

\[ \begin{aligned} \tau_{xx} &= \frac43 u_x - \frac23 v_y \\ \tau_{xy} &= u_y + v_x\\ \tau_{yy} &= \frac43 v_y - \frac23 u_x\\ \end{aligned} \]

还有

\[ \begin{aligned} \epsilon_1 &= u \tau_{xx} + v \tau_{xy}\\ &= u\left(\frac43 u_x - \frac23 v_y\right) + v\left(u_y + v_x\right)\\ \epsilon_2 &= u \tau_{xy} + v \tau_{yy}\\ &= u \left(u_y + v_x\right) + v\left(\frac43 v_y - \frac23 u_x\right) \end{aligned} \]

引入中间变量\(V_1 = U_x,V_2 = U_y\)，得到方程组

\[ \begin{aligned} U_t + F_1(U)_x +F_2(U)_y &= H_1(U,V_1,V_2)_x + H_2(U,V_1,V_2)_y + S(U)\\ V_1 &= G_1(U)_x\\ V_2 &= G_2(U)_y\\ \end{aligned} \]

其中\(G_1(U)=G_2(U)=U\)。记\(V_1=[\eta_1,\eta_2,\eta_3,\eta_4]^T\)，\(V_2=[\zeta_1,\zeta_2,\zeta_3,\zeta_4]^T\)，

对x方向，从原始变量转化到守恒型变量

\[ \begin{aligned} \eta_1 &= \rho_x \\ \eta_2 &= (\rho u)_x = \rho_x u + \rho u_x\\ \eta_3 &= (\rho v)_x = \rho_x v + \rho v_x\\ \eta_4 &= E_x = \frac{p_x}{\gamma-1} + \frac12 \rho_x(u^2+v^2) + \rho(u u_x + v v_x) \end{aligned} \]

从守恒型变量转化到原始变量

\[ \begin{aligned} \rho_x &= \eta_1 \\ u_x &= \frac{1}{\rho}(\eta_2 - \rho_x u) = \frac{1}{\xi_1}(\eta_2 - \eta_1 \frac{\xi_2}{\xi_1})\\ v_x &= \frac{1}{\rho}(\eta_3 - \rho_x v) = \frac{1}{\xi_1}(\eta_3 - \eta_1 \frac{\xi_3}{\xi_1})\\ p_x &= (\gamma-1)\left( \eta_4 - \frac12 \rho_x(u^2+v^2) - \rho(u u_x + v v_x) \right)\\ &= (\gamma-1)\left\{\eta_4 - \frac12 \eta_1 \left(\frac{\xi_2^2}{\xi_1^2} +\frac{\xi_3^2}{\xi_1^2}\right) - \xi_2 \frac{1}{\xi_1}(\eta_2 - \eta_1 \frac{\xi_2}{\xi_1}) - \xi_3 \frac{1}{\xi_1}(\eta_3 - \eta_1 \frac{\xi_3}{\xi_1}) \right\} \end{aligned} \]

对y方向完全类似，从原始变量转化到守恒型变量

\[ \begin{aligned} \zeta_1 &= \rho_y \\ \zeta_2 &= (\rho u)_y = \rho_y u + \rho u_y\\ \zeta_3 &= (\rho v)_y = \rho_y v + \rho v_y\\ \zeta_4 &= E_y = \frac{p_y}{\gamma-1} + \frac12 \rho_y(u^2+v^2) + \rho(u u_y + v v_y) \end{aligned} \]

从守恒型变量转化到原始变量

\[ \begin{aligned} \rho_y &= \zeta_1 \\ u_y &= \frac{1}{\rho}(\zeta_2 - \rho_y u) = \frac{1}{\xi_1}(\zeta_2 - \zeta_1 \frac{\xi_2}{\xi_1})\\ v_y &= \frac{1}{\rho}(\zeta_3 - \rho_y v) = \frac{1}{\xi_1}(\zeta_3 - \zeta_1 \frac{\xi_3}{\xi_1})\\ p_y &= (\gamma-1)\left( \zeta_4 - \frac12 \rho_y(u^2+v^2) - \rho(u u_y + v v_y) \right)\\ &= (\gamma-1)\left\{\zeta_4 - \frac12 \zeta_1 \left(\frac{\xi_2^2}{\xi_1^2} +\frac{\xi_3^2}{\xi_1^2}\right) - \xi_2 \frac{1}{\xi_1}(\zeta_2 - \zeta_1 \frac{\xi_2}{\xi_1}) - \xi_3 \frac{1}{\xi_1}(\zeta_3 - \zeta_1 \frac{\xi_3}{\xi_1}) \right\} \end{aligned} \]

在二维矩形网格下，DG半离散格式为：find \(U_h,V_{1,h},V_{2,h} \in V_h^k\), s.t. \(\forall W_h,Y_{1,h},Y_{2,h} \in V_h^k\)

\[ \begin{aligned} \int_{I_{ij}} (U_h)_t W_h &= \int_{I_{ij}}\left[ T_1 (W_h)_x + T_2 (W_h)_y \right] - \int_{\partial I_{ij}} \left[\widehat{T}_1 W_h + \widehat{T}_2 W_h\right] + \int_{I_{ij}} S(U_h) W_h \\ \int_{I_{ij}} V_{1,h} Y_{1,h} &= - \int_{I_{ij}}G_1(U_h) (Y_{1,h})_x + \int_{\partial I_{ij}} \widehat{G}_1(U_h) V_{1,h} \\ \int_{I_{ij}} V_{2,h} Y_{2,h} &= - \int_{I_{ij}}G_2(U_h) (Y_{2,h})_x + \int_{\partial I_{ij}} \widehat{G}_2(U_h) V_{2,h} \\ \end{aligned} \]

其中简记\(T_1(U_h,V_{1,h},V_{2,h}),T_2(U_h,V_{1,h},V_{2,h})\)如下

\[ \begin{aligned} T_1(U_h,V_{1,h},V_{2,h})= F_1(U_h)- H_1(U_h,V_{1,h},V_{2,h})\\ T_2(U_h,V_{1,h},V_{2,h})= F_2(U_h)- H_2(U_h,V_{1,h},V_{2,h})\\ \end{aligned} \]

二维的线性算例

计算区域\([0,2]^2\)，周期边界。

\[ \begin{aligned} \rho(x,y,0) &= 1+0.2\sin(\pi(x+y))\\ u(x,y,0)&= u_0 = 0.5\\ u(x,y,0)&= u_0 = 0.5\\ p(x,y,0)&= p_0 = 1\\ \end{aligned} \]

取常数\(\gamma=1.4\)，此时对于Euler方程组，恰好时间项和对流项抵消，退化为线性方程，精确解为

\[ \begin{aligned} \rho(x,y,t) &= 1+0.2\sin(\pi(x+y - (u_0 + v_0) t))\\ u(x,y,t)&= u_0 = 0.5\\ u(x,y,t)&= u_0 = 0.5\\ p(x,y,t)&= p_0 = 1\\ \end{aligned} \]

因此，我们加上源项，直接和黏性项抵消。对于黏性项，取\(Re=1000\)，\(Pr=0.72\)，则黏性项和源项为

\[ \begin{aligned} H_1(U,U_x,U_y) &= \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 0\\ 0\\ \kappa \Phi^{(1,4)}(x,y,t) \end{pmatrix}\\ H_2(U,U_x,U_y) &= \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 0\\ 0\\ \kappa \Phi^{(2,4)}(x,y,t) \end{pmatrix}\\ S(x,y,t) &= \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 0\\ 0\\ -\kappa \Phi^{(1,4)}(x,y,t)_x - \kappa \Phi^{(2,4)}(x,y,t)_y \end{pmatrix}\\ \end{aligned} \]

其中常系数 \(\kappa = \frac{\gamma}{Re\,Pr(\gamma-1)}\)，\(\Phi^{(1,4)}_x\)和\(\Phi^{(2,4)}_y\)具体如下

\[ \begin{aligned} \Phi^{(1,4)}(x,y,t)_x &= p_0\left(\frac{1}{\rho}\right)_{xx} = p_0 \left( \frac{2\rho_x^2}{\rho^3} - \frac{\rho_{xx}}{\rho^2} \right)\\ \Phi^{(2,4)}(x,y,t)_y &= p_0\left(\frac{1}{\rho}\right)_{yy} = p_0 \left( \frac{2\rho_y^2}{\rho^3} - \frac{\rho_{yy}}{\rho^2} \right)\\ \end{aligned} \]

计算结果

对于k=0,1,2，都达到了预期的精度，注意对于k=0,1,2，使用了\(\Delta t = \frac{0.5}{2k+3} \left(\frac{\Delta x^2}{\alpha}\right)\)，终止时间\(t=0.2\)。如下图：
